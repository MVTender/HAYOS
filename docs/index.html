FAQ

Good to know

Is HAYOS really free?

Yes — 100% free, forever. No ads, no account, no trial limits. Donations are optional.

Where are my files stored?

On your own computer, only. HAYOS works fully offline and never uploads anything.

What formats can I open and save?

Open .hys, .txt, .md, .html and .rtf. Save to the native .hys, or export to Word, RTF, Markdown, TXT, HTML and PDF.

Is it safe to install?

Yes. HAYOS is a lightweight launcher around your own files and the built-in Windows WebView2. The installer needs no admin rights. As it's a new indie app, Windows SmartScreen may show a notice — click “More info → Run anyway”.

Does it work on Mac or Linux?

Right now HAYOS is built for Windows 10 and 11. Other platforms may come later.
